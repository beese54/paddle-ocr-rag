RAG Pipeline - Next Steps (Steps 3-5)                                                                                                                                                                       
  === STEP 3: Tune Retrieval Quality ===                                                               
  In rag_chain.py, you can:
  - Increase k if answers seem to miss context: search_kwargs={"k": 8}
  - Switch to gpt-4o-mini to reduce cost once happy with quality: model="gpt-4o-mini"

  === STEP 4: Deploy with Docker (already set up) ===
  The project already has a Dockerfile and docker-compose.yml. When ready to share:
    docker-compose up
  You'll need to mount your chroma_db/ or re-run ingest inside the container.

  | Improvement                              | Why
